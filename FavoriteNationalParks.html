My Favorite National Parks!

Map 1:

My favorite National Park I have visited is Rocky Mountain National Park. I love this National Park because of its diversity in species, biomes, and elevations to observe. When I visited this park I saw a lot of Moose which is my favorite animal to see in National Parks.

Map 2:

Second on my list is Zion National Park. While Zion didn't provide a ton of wildlife, its scenery is breathtaking. I got to hike in the Narrows which is a hike inside of a canyon in the Park. With amazing, daredevil-like hikes and out-of-this world views, Zion was a remarkable place to visit.

Map 3:

Lastly in my top 3 National Parks is Crater Lake National Park. Being from Oregon, Crater Lake has been a popular camping spot in my lifetime. The Park is not just the only National Park in Oregon, but a geological marvel seen nowhere else in the world. Not only is it geologically impressive, but the Park is located in a beautifully forested area of Oregon.
